# NLP & The Transformer Revolution

## Why This Matters

In 2017, a team at Google published a paper with a deceptively simple title: **"Attention Is All You Need."**

That paper changed everything.

Before it, teaching machines to understand language was slow, painful, and limited. After it, we got GPT, BERT, LLaMA, Claude, and every large language model that powers the AI revolution today.

If you want to be a GenAI engineer, understanding transformers isn't optional — it's the foundation everything else sits on. RAG, fine-tuning, prompt engineering, AI agents — all of it traces back to the transformer architecture.
